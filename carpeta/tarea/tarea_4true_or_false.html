Tarea 4:True or false

Pregunta Verdadero-Falso

True or false? A continuación vas a leer una serie de frases............ serán verdaderas o falsas?

Pregunta 1

Lions likes carrots and tomatoes. 

Retroalimentación

Falso

Remember lions are carnivorous animals, they like meat and eat others animals. 

Pregunta 2

Dogs and cats are pets. When they are toddlers they are called "Puppy" and " Kitten"

Retroalimentación

Verdadero

Remember babies animals...... 

Dog.... puppy

Cat..... kitten

Rabbit... bunny

Cow .... calf.
